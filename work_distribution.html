Work Distribution

Our method of communication during this project consisted of seminars (werkcolleges), meetings, and additional consultation over text. In our first meeting, each team member presented a dataset along with at least two different perspectives. We then chose the subject and data that best suited the requirements for the project. Our first step after determining the subject was to clean and merge the datasets from each year to form one singular dataset. We also started to discuss possible graphs that could be made to support the different perspectives. We then started making the graphs and worked on a story that builds a narrative around them, which led to our draft version. Furthermore, the remaining time has been used to further improve the graphs as well as the story. Finally, with the help of the given peer feedback, we created the final data story.
